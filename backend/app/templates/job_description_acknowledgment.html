1. General Responsibilities

As a home care worker with the Agency, my responsibilities may include but are not limited to:

  1. Personal Care: Assisting clients with activities of daily living (ADLs) including bathing, dressing, grooming, toileting, and mobility
  2. Companionship: Providing emotional support, conversation, and engagement in social activities
  3. Meal Preparation: Planning, preparing, and serving meals according to dietary requirements and preferences
  4. Light Housekeeping: Maintaining a clean, safe, and comfortable living environment for clients
  5. Medication Reminders: Reminding clients to take prescribed medications at scheduled times (not administering medications unless certified)
  6. Transportation: Providing or arranging transportation to appointments, errands, and social activities
  7. Documentation: Maintaining accurate records of care provided, observations, and client status changes
  8. Communication: Reporting changes in client condition to supervisors and care coordinators promptly

2. Physical Requirements

I understand that the position may require:

  • Standing, walking, bending, and lifting for extended periods
  • Lifting or moving up to 50 pounds (with or without assistance)
  • Assisting with client transfers and mobility
  • Working in various home environments

3. Professional Standards

I understand that I am expected to:

  • Maintain professional boundaries with clients and their families
  • Adhere to the Agency's code of ethics and conduct policies
  • Maintain required certifications and training throughout employment
  • Report to assigned shifts on time and communicate scheduling changes in advance
