This Confidentiality and Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by {{ applicant_name }} ("Employee/Applicant") and {{ company_name }} ("Agency").

1. Purpose

As a condition of employment or engagement with the Agency, the Employee/Applicant acknowledges that they may have access to confidential and proprietary information related to the Agency's clients, operations, and business practices.

2. Definition of Confidential Information

Confidential Information includes, but is not limited to:

  • Client personal information, medical records, and care plans
  • Client and family contact information and home addresses
  • Agency business strategies, financial data, and operational procedures
  • Employee records, compensation details, and personnel files
  • Proprietary software, systems, and technology used by the Agency
  • Any information designated as confidential by the Agency

3. Obligations

The Employee/Applicant agrees to:

  1. Maintain strict confidentiality of all Confidential Information during and after employment.
  2. Not disclose, publish, or otherwise reveal any Confidential Information to any third party without prior written authorization.
  3. Not use Confidential Information for personal gain or for the benefit of any entity other than the Agency.
  4. Take reasonable precautions to prevent unauthorized access to Confidential Information.
  5. Comply with all applicable HIPAA regulations regarding Protected Health Information (PHI).
  6. Return all materials containing Confidential Information upon termination of employment.

4. HIPAA Compliance

The Employee/Applicant understands that violations of HIPAA regulations may result in civil and criminal penalties, including fines up to $250,000 and imprisonment up to 10 years for certain violations.

5. Duration

This Agreement shall remain in effect during the Employee/Applicant's engagement with the Agency and shall survive the termination of such engagement indefinitely with respect to Confidential Information.

6. Breach

Any breach of this Agreement may result in immediate termination of employment and may subject the Employee/Applicant to legal action, including claims for damages and injunctive relief.

By signing below, I acknowledge that I have read, understand, and agree to abide by the terms of this Confidentiality and Non-Disclosure Agreement.
